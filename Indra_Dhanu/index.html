About
Indradhanu Waldorf School, located in Nungambakkam, Chennai, is the city's first institution to adopt the Waldorf educational philosophy. The school emphasizes holistic development, nurturing the head, heart, and hands of each child. Their approach integrates emotional, creative, and intellectual growth, ensuring a balanced education.
Committed to fostering imagination and sensory experiences, Indradhanu provides a nurturing environment that supports the physiological, emotional, intellectual, and spiritual development of its students. The school plans to affiliate its high school with the IGCSE Cambridge International curriculum.
History
Established in 2012, Indradhanu Waldorf School began as a modest kindergarten class with just five students in a two-room apartment. Over the years, the school has grown in size but remains steadfast in its commitment to joyful learning and the well-being of each child.
The institution's evolution reflects its dedication to providing an environment where laughter and joy are integral to education. This growth underscores the school's mission to offer a nurturing space that prioritizes the child's holistic development.
Curriculum
Indradhanu's curriculum is rooted in the Waldorf philosophy, emphasizing structured yet flexible learning that aligns with a child's developmental stages. The Early Childhood Programme caters to children aged 24 months to 6 years, focusing on learning through imitation and repetition in a home-like setting. Activities such as singing, painting, and movement are integral, fostering organic learning without formal instruction.
As students progress to the Grade Programme, the curriculum evolves to include more structured academic content while maintaining the Waldorf emphasis on creativity and sensory engagement. Subjects are taught through artistic expression, movement, and hands-on activities, ensuring a comprehensive educational experience.
Admission
Admissions at Indradhanu Waldorf School are open from Nursery to Grade 9. The Early Childhood Programme is divided into Nursery (24 months to 3½ years) and Kindergarten (3½ to 6 years). The school emphasizes a non-competitive, technology-free environment, focusing on holistic development without the use of textbooks, homework, or formal assessments.
Interested parents can contact the school directly via phone or email to inquire about the admission process and schedule a visit. The school also hosts Open House events, providing an opportunity for families to experience the Waldorf approach firsthand.
FAQ
  1. What age groups does Indradhanu Waldorf School cater to?
    The school offers programs from Nursery (24 months) up to Grade 9.
  2. Does the school use technology in classrooms?
    No, the school maintains a technology-free environment to encourage sensory and imaginative development.
  3. Are there formal assessments or homework?
    The school does not use traditional assessments or homework, focusing instead on holistic, experiential learning.
  4. How can I learn more about the school's approach?
    Prospective parents are encouraged to attend Open House events or contact the school directly for more information.
