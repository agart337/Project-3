About
Prerana Waldorf School, located in Gachibowli, Hyderabad, offers a holistic education that nurtures the head, heart, and hands. Emphasizing the protection of childhood, the school provides an integrated and age-appropriate curriculum that celebrates social and cultural diversity.
As a CBSE-affiliated institution, Prerana combines academic rigor with Waldorf educational principles, fostering intellectual, emotional, and practical development. The school's mission is rooted in promoting healthy human development and nurturing the essence of childhood as the foundation of a truly human culture. With a commitment to inclusivity and respect for individuality, Prerana creates a supportive environment where students can thrive. The school is proud to be ranked among the Top 20 schools in Telangana. ​
History
Established in 2001 by Indira and Maj Varma, Prerana Waldorf School began as a kindergarten and nursery, gradually expanding by adding one grade each year. The founders were inspired by the need for an education system that nurtures the sanctity of childhood, leading them to adopt the Waldorf educational philosophy developed by Rudolf Steiner.
This approach emphasizes holistic development and social responsibility. Initially located in Banjara Hills, the school later moved to its own campus in Gachibowli, evolving into a dynamic community serving students up to Grade XII. Prerana meets all CBSE board requirements while incorporating Waldorf methodologies up to Grade VIII.
Curriculum
Prerana's curriculum is designed to align with the developmental stages of children, offering a balanced education that integrates academics, arts, and practical skills. In the early years, learning is experiential, focusing on play and imitation.
Grades 1-8 introduce reading and writing through storytelling and imaginative activities, while high school emphasizes cognitive development and critical thinking. Subjects are taught in thematic blocks, allowing in-depth exploration and integration across disciplines. The curriculum includes physical training, arts, and science labs, ensuring a well-rounded education. Assessments are narrative-based, providing comprehensive insights into each student's progress.

FAQ
Browse below to see our most frequently asked questions. Click here for answers to these questions.
  1. How is Waldorf education different from other educational systems?
    Waldorf education is child-centered, meeting children at their developmental stage and nurturing their intellectual, emotional, and practical capacities. It emphasizes holistic development over early academic pressure. ​
  2. Is Waldorf similar to Montessori?
    While both emphasize developmentally appropriate, hands-on learning, Montessori focuses on early academics and individual achievement, whereas Waldorf prioritizes imagination and social collaboration.
  3. Is Waldorf education religious?
    Waldorf schools are non-sectarian and non-denominational. They honor the spiritual dimension of life without adhering to any specific religious doctrine, celebrating festivals from various traditions. ​
