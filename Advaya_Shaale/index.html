About
Advaya Shaale is a coeducational institution rooted in the principles of Waldorf education, adapted to the Indian context. Established in 2015, the school emphasizes holistic development by engaging the hands, heart, and head of each child.
The curriculum is designed to foster an intrinsic love for learning, focusing on goodness, beauty, and truth. Located in Kasturi Nagar, Bangalore, Advaya Shaale serves students from Grades 1 to 8. The school community, comprising parents, teachers, and students, collaborates to create a nurturing environment that promotes self-learning and harmonious growth.
History
Advaya Shaale was founded in 2015 through the collective efforts of two Waldorf Kindergartens—Promise Centre at Babusapalya and Prakriti at Indiranagar—and an enthusiastic parent community. The school began with a vision to bring Waldorf education to urban children in Bangalore. Starting with a modest setup, it has grown over the years, reflecting a commitment to holistic education. The school's foundation is inspired by the educational philosophy of Dr. Rudolf Steiner, aiming to educate the whole child by reaching their heart, will, and mind.
Curriculum
Advaya Shaale's curriculum is based on the Waldorf education model, emphasizing age-appropriate learning that aligns with the natural development of the child. The teaching methodology incorporates storytelling, movement, artistic activities, and practical experiences to make learning engaging and meaningful.
Subjects are taught imaginatively, integrating artistic elements even in academic topics. This approach nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and emotional intelligence, aiming to develop well-rounded individuals. The curriculum also emphasizes rhythm and repetition, providing a structured yet flexible learning environment.
Admission
The admission process for Advaya Shaale begins in the month of September with an open day organised for all interested parents. Herein we explain the broad elements of Waldorf education and dwell briefly on the Steiner philosophy. It is recommended that both parents are present for the Open Day.
Those interested in joining the school are called in for a face to face with the Advaya Management Team on another day. It is compulsory for both parents to be present for this meeting. The admission form is handed over to the parents at the end of this meeting. After which, closer to the month of November we meet with the children for an observation to understand the child better. Parents are required to submit the completed admission form on this day.
