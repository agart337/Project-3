About
Shining Spiral Ahmedabad is Gujarat’s first Waldorf-inspired kindergarten, recognized by the International Association for Steiner/Waldorf Early Childhood Education (IASWECE). The school blends the international Waldorf curriculum with culturally rich traditional Indian wisdom, creating an environment conducive to the healthy development of children aged 0 to 7. Emphasizing the 'dignity of childhood,' Shining Spiral offers programs from Playgroup to Grade 1, focusing on warmth, truth, and goodness.
The school's vision is to steer minds capable of molding their lives meaningfully and coexisting harmoniously with nature. Their mission is to nurture skills of observance, independence, resourcefulness, and reverence by providing a rested childhood with age-appropriate activities.
History
Established as the first Waldorf-inspired kindergarten in Gujarat, Shining Spiral Ahmedabad was founded to provide an educational environment that respects the natural development of children. The school integrates the international Waldorf curriculum with traditional Indian cultural values, aiming to create a harmonious learning experience.
Established as the first Waldorf-inspired kindergarten in Gujarat, Shining Spiral Ahmedabad was founded to provide an educational environment that respects the natural development of children. The school integrates the international Waldorf curriculum with traditional Indian cultural values, aiming to create a harmonious learning experience. Recognized by IASWECE, Shining Spiral has been a pioneer in introducing Waldorf education principles in the region. Over the years, it has grown to include programs up to Grade 1, maintaining its commitment to nurturing the 'dignity of childhood.'
Curriculum
Shining Spiral's curriculum is designed to educate the whole child—head, heart, and hands—aligning with Waldorf education principles. For children aged 0 to 7, the focus is on providing a warm, secure environment that encourages imaginative play and creativity.
Activities include storytelling, puppet plays, painting, beading, and working with natural materials like beeswax and wool. The curriculum emphasizes age-appropriate rhythms, daily routines, and exposure to nature, fostering a sense of wonder and reverence. This holistic approach aims to develop socially and professionally capable adults.
Admission
Shining Spiral Ahmedabad accepts admissions for Playgroup, Nursery, Jr. KG, Sr. KG, and Grade 1. Interested parents can inquire through the school's website or contact them via email or phone. The admission process involves understanding the school's philosophy and ensuring alignment with the family's educational values. Parents are encouraged to visit the school to experience the environment firsthand. The school emphasizes creating a partnership with parents to support the child's development.
