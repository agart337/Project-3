About
Diksha – A Waldorf School is a Hyderabad-based educational institution that has been offering Waldorf Education for 17 years. As part of the global community of Waldorf schools, Diksha is committed to nurturing minds, stimulating intellects, and touching the lives of children. The school emphasizes holistic development, aiming to cultivate individuals who seek and add meaning to their own lives and contribute positively to society.
Diksha's approach integrates academic learning with artistic, practical, and social activities, fostering a well-rounded education. The school's environment encourages creativity, critical thinking, and emotional intelligence, aligning with the core principles of Waldorf education.
History
Established 17 years ago, Diksha – A Waldorf School has grown to become a cherished part of the global Waldorf education movement. The school's foundation was rooted in the desire to provide an alternative educational approach that focuses on the holistic development of children.
Over the years, Diksha has expanded its programs and facilities, continually adapting to meet the evolving needs of its students while staying true to the principles of Waldorf education. The school's commitment to nurturing individuality and fostering a love for learning has remained steadfast throughout its history.
Curriculum
Diksha's curriculum is based on the Waldorf education model, which emphasizes a holistic approach to learning. The curriculum is designed to cater to the developmental stages of children, integrating academic subjects with arts, crafts, music, and movement.
This approach aims to educate the "head, heart, and hands," fostering intellectual, emotional, and practical skills. The school offers programs from Kindergarten through Upper School, ensuring a continuous and comprehensive educational journey. Teachers at Diksha are trained to deliver the curriculum in a manner that inspires curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning.
Admission
Diksha welcomes admissions across various grade levels.
